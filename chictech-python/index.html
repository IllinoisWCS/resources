Ice Breaker

What is Data Science?

What is Data?

facts and statistics collected together for reference or analysis

Source: Google

Big Data: Why It Matters

  • 90% of the world's data was generated in the last 2-3 years
  • 2.5 billion gigabytes of data were generated every day in 2012 - according to IBM
  • Between Google, Amazon, Microsoft and Facebook, it is estimated that there's 1,200 petabytes of data just between them - ScienceFocus
  • Global data amounts to 1 zettabyte - 1000^7 bytes - 152 million years of HD video

Data Science

  • Hybrid between Statistics, Computer Science and Business
  • Gleaning insights from this valuable data to make informed decisions
  • Visualizing data in such a way to make sense of it all

How is Data Science used?

A/B testing - web applications with different features

Consumer recommendations - Amazon, Netflix, etc.

Consumer trends - How people use certain products

FastCompany - The Best (and Worst) Times to Post on Social Media

Revenue opportunities - ad targeting, investor/stock forecasting

Technologies to "do" Data Science

  • Programming Languages - Python, R (statistical computing)
  • Big Data Frameworks - Hadoop, Spark, Pig
  • Concepts - Map-Reduce, Aggregation, Regression, Distributions
  • Visualization Frameworks - Matplotlib, D3, Processing
