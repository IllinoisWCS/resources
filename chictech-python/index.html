What is Data?

Source: Google

Big Data: Why It Matters

  • 90% of the world's data was generated in the last 2-3 years
  • 2.5 billion gigabytes of data were generated every day in 2012 - according to IBM
  • Global data amounts to 1 zettabyte - 1000^7 bytes - 152 million years of HD video

Data Science

  • Hybrid between Statistics, Computer Science and Business
  • Gleaning insights from this valuable data to make informed decisions
  • Visualizing data in such a way to make sense of it all
